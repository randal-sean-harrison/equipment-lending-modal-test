        Borrowing Policies

        Please review the following guidelines regarding the reservation, pick-up, and return of equipment:

        • Reservation Timeline — Request equipment at least one business day in advance.
        • Loan Limits — Reserve items in daily increments for up to 7 days, booking as early as two weeks in advance.
        • Pick-Up Location — Once you receive the confirmation email, retrieve your item from the 1st Floor Equipment Lending Hold Shelf (located behind the Circulation Desk).
        • Retrieval Process — Look for the brown paper bag with your reservation slip; items are pre-checked, so there is no need to use the self-check machine.
        • Returns — Return the item in its original bag to the Hold Shelf by 5:00pm on your reservation end date.

        If you have questions, require special arrangements, or encounter issues with your return, please contact us at cds@nd.edu.
